## Download Instructions

### Android/iOS

1.  Visit the Google Play Store (Android) or the App Store (iOS).
2.  Search for "Quotex Money Management."
3.  Tap the "Install" button.
4.  Once the installation is complete, open the app.
5.  Tap the "Download Excel Sheet" button.

### PC

1.  Visit the Quotex website.
2.  Click on the "Resources" tab.
3.  Click on the "Excel Sheet" link.
4.  Click on the "Download" button.
5.  Save the file to your computer.
